How to Apply at Motjere Secondary School

1. Required Documents

Motjere Secondary School requires the following documents for new learners:

  • Certified Copy of Child's Birth Certificate
  • Parent or Guardian's ID
  • Proof of Residence (municipal bill or affidavit)
  • Last Report Card (if transferring from another school)
2. Application Steps

Visit the School:

Go directly to Motjere Secondary School to submit the application.

Complete the Application Form:

Fill out the specific application form provided by the school and attach the required documents.

About Motjere Secondary School

Motjere Secondary School, situated amidst the rural landscapes of the Limpopo Province, is a public secondary school offering ordinary education.

Located at Lapucella Village, Ga-Matlala in the vibrant suburb of Lekhureng it stands as a beacon of dedicated learning within the Limpopo community.

School Fees

Motjere Secondary School operates as a no-fee institution under South Africa's policy, which ensures education accessibility regardless of financial status.

Government funding covers essential expenses, eliminating fees to promote inclusivity and equal opportunities for all learners, facilitating quality education environments conducive to academic success.
